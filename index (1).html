The Author

Verona

V

Verona writes psychological fiction set in the dark corners of European cities — where obsession masquerades as love and everyone is one secret away from collapse.

Love Out of List is her debut work, built from a fascination with the way people orbit each other without ever truly colliding. She writes about desire, manipulation, and the things we do when we think no one is watching.
